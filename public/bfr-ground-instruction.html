BFR Ground Instruction

Already your own pilot in command but need to take your BFR?

Already have a pilot certificate, but need a flight review? Save money and do the ground portion with us! Then when you go to your CFI, you only have to pay for the time with them. We will issue your endorsement for the ground portion and get you on your way as quickly as possible! Even better yet, all you need is your logbook!

Do I need a BFR?
  • If you have not successfully completed an additional check ride or BFR within the preceding 24 months, you are in need of a BFR. If you have any questions about whether you need a BFR or not, send us a message!

Required Materials:
  • Paper and pencil
  • Pilot logbook

Please do not forget your logbook. The BFR normally only takes one session and we cannot issue your endorsement without the book.
