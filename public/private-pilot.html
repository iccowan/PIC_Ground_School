Private Pilot Course

Becoming a private pilot is the first step in becoming your own pilot in command.

The private pilot certificate will most likely be the most rewarding certificate you will earn on your journey. During the private pilot ground school course you will learn the basic fundamentals of flight and how to control the airplane. You will learn about all of the systems of the airplane and the weather that will effect every one of your flights. Join us and allow us to assist you in becoming a competent pilot and in passing the knowledge exam with ease!

Required Materials:
  • Paper and pencil
  • Private Pilot FAA Knowledge Exam Review, Gleim Aviation (ISBN: 1618541277)
  • The Complete Private Pilot, Bob Gardner (ISBN: 1619543222)
  • ​FAR AIM 2018, ASA (ISBN: 1619545365)
  • VFR Sectional for YOUR section*
  • Chart Supplement U.S. for YOUR section*
  • E6B Flight Computer
  • VFR Sectional Plotter
  • Course syllabus (you will receive this through email or on the first day)
  • Pilot logbook

*If you have an EFB substitute (ForeFlight, Garmin Pilot, etc.) these may be used instead of paper charts although it is always good to have backup paper charts.

Please have these materials on the first session as they will be used throughout the course as references and study material. If you have any questions about the material, contact the instructors on the "contact" page.
